# Contact

Please feel free to contact us in case of any question related to the API and/or any other technical question you may have, using the following email addresses:

- Romanian customer support: `suport@minicrm.ro`
- Hungarian customer support: `help@minicrm.hu`
- International customer support — please write in English: `help-en@minicrm.io`

Also, if you use MiniCRM, you will find our phone number directly in your MiniCRM account by clicking the button in the top right corner.

The phone numbers are specific to each country and can change over time — this is why we did not include them here.

If you are a developer and you are writing to us on behalf of a customer, please mention the customer's name or their company name in your email.
